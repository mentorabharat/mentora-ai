What is a Percentage?

Percentage means "per hundred". It represents a part of a whole out of 100.

Basic Formula

Percentage = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100

Example 1 – Exam Marks

Marks: 45 out of 60

  • Part → 45
  • Whole → 60
  • Percentage → (45 ÷ 60) × 100
  • → 0.75 × 100 = 75%
Final Percentage = 75%

Example 2 – Discount

Price: ₹1000

Discount: 20%

  • 20% of 1000 → (20 ÷ 100) × 1000
  • → 0.20 × 1000 = ₹200
  • Final Price → 1000 – 200 = ₹800
You Pay = ₹800

Example 3 – Increase

Salary: ₹10,000

Increase: 10%

  • 10% of 10,000 → ₹1,000
  • New Salary → ₹11,000
New Salary = ₹11,000

Quick Mental Tricks

  • 50% = Half
  • 25% = One-fourth
  • 10% = Move decimal left
  • 1% = Divide by 100
