Averages

What is an Average?

An average is a way to find a central value from a group of numbers. It helps us understand what is typical or expected.

Why Averages Matter

  • Helps simplify many values into one meaningful number
  • Used in exams, daily life, business, and data analysis
  • Makes comparison easier and fair
