What is an Average?

An average represents a central value of a group of numbers. It shows the overall level instead of individual variations.

Basic Formula

Average = Sum of Values ÷ Number of Values

Example 1 – Simple Numbers

Numbers: 10, 20, 30

  • Sum → 10 + 20 + 30 = 60
  • Count → 3
  • Average → 60 ÷ 3 = 20
Final Average = 20

Example 2 – Marks of Students

Marks: 45, 55, 65, 35

  • Sum → 45 + 55 + 65 + 35 = 200
  • Count → 4
  • Average → 200 ÷ 4 = 50
Average Marks = 50

Example 3 – Daily Life Understanding

If you travel 3 days:

Distances: 20 km, 30 km, 10 km

  • Total Distance → 60 km
  • Days → 3
  • Average per Day → 60 ÷ 3 = 20 km
Average Distance = 20 km/day
