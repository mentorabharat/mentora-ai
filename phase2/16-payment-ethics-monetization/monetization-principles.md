# Monetization Principles

Mentora follows these principles:

1. Education first, income second
2. No compulsory payment to learn basics
3. No manipulation, fear, or urgency tactics
4. No ads inside learning flow
5. Transparency in every paid offering

Revenue must come from:
- Value
- Trust
- Long-term usage

Not from:
- Addiction
- Artificial scarcity
- Algorithmic pressure
