# Mentora World AI — Boundaries (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

## What Mentora IS
- Education & guidance ecosystem
- Decision-support system
- AI-assisted but human-governed platform
- Open + original knowledge builder

## What Mentora IS NOT
- ❌ Coaching class
- ❌ Course-selling marketplace
- ❌ Autonomous AI decision-maker
- ❌ Content piracy platform
- ❌ Political / religious ideology promoter

## Education Boundaries
- Civil education only
- No defence / military / classified training
- No extremist or banned ideology
- No illegal or unethical instruction

## AI Boundaries
- No autonomous AI decisions
- No self-updating AI in Phase-2
- No AI publishing without human approval
- No AI copying paid or copyrighted content

## Country & Policy Boundaries
- Country-wise data separation
- Local education laws respected
- High-risk countries can be disabled
- Pakistan — permanently excluded

## Monetization Boundaries (Phase-2)
- ❌ No payment execution
- ❌ No monetization logic
- ✅ Only structural mapping

> If something violates these boundaries,
> it does not belong in Mentora World AI.
