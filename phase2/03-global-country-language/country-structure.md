## Core Principles
- Each country is a separate logical container
- Data does NOT mix across countries
- Local education policies are respected
- Features can be enabled/disabled per country

## India-First Strategy
- India is the first active country
- Initial focus: civil education systems
- Languages: Marathi, Hindi, English (Phase-2 scope)

## Global Expansion Model
- Countries added in controlled batches
- No auto-enablement
- Manual review before activation

## High-Risk Country Policy
- Mentora may restrict or block countries
- Based on legal, ethical, or security risks

- Pakistan is permanently excluded
- No access, no data, no services
