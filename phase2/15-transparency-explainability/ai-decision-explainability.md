# AI Decision Explainability

AI explanations in Mentora World AI are:

Allowed:
- Conceptual reasoning
- Educational logic
- Topic relevance explanation
- Learning path justification

Not Allowed:
- Raw model weights
- Internal prompts
- Security-sensitive logic
- Private user data exposure

Explainability Level:
- Student: Simple & educational
- Mentor: Structured & instructional
- Founder/Admin: System-level overview

AI explains outcomes, not internal machinery.
