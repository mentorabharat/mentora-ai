# Trust Principles

Mentora World AI is built on *earned trust*, not forced control.

## Core Principles
- Education-first, not surveillance-first
- Calm learning environment
- No fear-based moderation
- No algorithmic punishment
- Human authority always above AI

## What Builds Trust
- Transparent boundaries
- Predictable system behavior
- Founder-visible governance
- No hidden penalties
- No shadow banning

Trust is treated as a *long-term asset*, not a metric.
