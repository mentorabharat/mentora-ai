Ratio

What is a Ratio?

A ratio compares two or more quantities to show their relative size. It tells us how much of one thing there is compared to another.

Why Ratios Matter

  • Used in exams, recipes, maps, speed, and sharing problems
  • Helps understand relationships, not just values
  • Makes comparison simple and logical

Basic Representation

Ratio of A to B is written as A : B
Example: If apples = 2 and oranges = 3, ratio = 2 : 3
