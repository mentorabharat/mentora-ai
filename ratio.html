What is a Ratio?

Ratio compares two quantities. It shows how much of one thing exists relative to another.

Ratio = Quantity A : Quantity B

Example 1 – Simple Comparison

Boys: 10

Girls: 5

  • Ratio → 10 : 5
  • Simplify → Divide by 5
  • → 2 : 1
Final Ratio = 2 : 1

Example 2 – Sharing Money

Total Money: ₹900

Ratio: 2 : 1

  • Total parts → 2 + 1 = 3
  • Value of one part → 900 ÷ 3 = ₹300
  • Person A → 2 × 300 = ₹600
  • Person B → 1 × 300 = ₹300
Distribution = ₹600 & ₹300

Example 3 – Mixture Problem

Milk : Water = 3 : 1

  • Total parts → 4
  • Milk fraction → 3/4
  • Water fraction → 1/4
Milk = 75% • Water = 25%

How to Simplify Ratios

  • Find common divisor
  • Divide both numbers
  • Keep comparison intact
