What is a Ratio?

A ratio compares two quantities and shows their relationship. It helps understand proportional differences between values.

Basic Representation

Ratio = Value A : Value B

Example

Suppose we compare:

10 and 20

  • Step 1: Write → 10 : 20
  • Step 2: Simplify → Divide both by 10
Final Ratio = 1 : 2

Where Ratios Are Used

  • Mathematics & exams
  • Finance & comparisons
  • Business analysis
  • Daily life decisions
